Undergraduate Students

I regularly mentor undergraduate students who are interested in gaining research experience. Student positions with me are either to learn molecular lab skills or data analysis. I offer in depth, progressive research experiences and I want students to gain strong scientific skills while working with me. Students who work with me for multiple years typically do a senior thesis project . I usually recruit students through the URAP program, where I advertise available positions. Students who are particularly interested in my research can email me with their interests and I will let you know if any student positions are likely to be available soon.
