I am a staff researcher working at the Museum of Vertebrate Zoology at U.C. Berkeley.  My fields of study include evolutionary biology, behavioral ecology, herpetology, ornithology, and population genetics.  I am particularly interested in questions pertaining to the genetic basis of adaptive traits, plasticity, speciation, sexual selection, and the maintenance and loss of polymorphism.  Please browse my website to learn more about my research!
